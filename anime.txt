ROMANTIC
    -Tora-dora:
        A high school story about Ryuuji and Taiga,
        two students with opposite personalities
        who team up to help each other with their crushes,
        only to gradually grow closer themselves.
        A heartwarming mix of comedy,romance, and friendship.
    -Fly me to the Moon:
        A young man falls in love at first sight and
        marries a mysterious girl, despite knowing almost nothing about her.
        Filled with cute, funny, and heartwarming moments of everyday life.
ISEKAI:
    -Reincarnation of jobless:
        A man dies and is reborn in a fantasy world with a chance
        to fix his past mistakes. Focuses on magic, adventure,
        and the personal growth of the main character.
    -About how I became a slime:
        The main character dies and is reincarnated as a slime
        in a fantasy world, gradually gaining powers and building his own kingdom.
        A fun blend of humor, action, and world-building.
